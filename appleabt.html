Apple Computer Inc.


Apple Computer Inc., is an American multinational corporation that designs and manufactures consumer electronics and software products

The company's best-known hardware products include Macintosh computers, the iPod and the iPhone.

Apple software includes the Mac OS X operating system, the iTunes media browser, the iLife suite of multimedia and creativity software, the iWork suite of productivity software

The company operates several hundred retail stores in a number of countries as well as the online online store and iTunes Store
